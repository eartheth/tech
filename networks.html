Types of Networks

Placeholders only. Replace with your own non-empty descriptions for PAN, LAN, WAN, WLAN, etc.

PAN

Personal Area Network

  • Use: Communication between personal devices in a short range
  • Technology: Bluetooth, Wi-Fi
LAN

Local Area Network

  • Use: connect computers and devices win a small area to share resources
  • Technology: Ethernet and Wi-Fi
WAN

Wide Area Network

  • Use: connect computer networks across cities, countries, and the world
  • Technology: satellites
WLAN

Wireless Local Area Network

  • Use: connect devices in a limited area without physical cables
  • Technology: high-frequency radio waves
MAN

Metropolitan Area Network

  • Use: connect multiple LANs across a city or large metropolitan area, providing high-speed data
  • Technology: Fiber optic cables
CAN

Campus Area Network

  • Use: connect multiple LANs in a campus
  • Technology: Ethernet, Wifi 6
SAN

Storage Area Network

  • Use: connect servers to a network of storage devices
  • Technology: databases
POLAN

Passive Optical Local Area Network

  • Use: LANs for tall, big buildings
  • Technology: IoT devices, sensors
EPN

Enterprise Private Network

  • Use: securely connect an organization's branches
  • Technology: LANs, WANs, cloud services
VPN

Virtual Private Network

  • Use: securing internet connections by IP address masking
  • Technology: 1.1.1.1
SAN

System Area Network

  • Use: high-speed network that provides multiple servers
  • Technology: fast data backup and recovery
