Types of Networks

Do you know all of these types of network?

PAN

Personal Area Network

  • Use: Communication between personal devices in a short range
  • Technology: Bluetooth, Wi-Fi
LAN

Local Area Network

  • Use: connect computers and devices in a small area
  • Technology: Ethernet, Wi-Fi
WAN

Wide Area Network

  • Use: connect computer networks across cities, countries, and the world
  • Technology: Satellites
WLAN

Wireless LAN

  • Use: connect devices without physical cables
  • Technology: High-frequency radio waves
MAN

Metropolitan Area Network

  • Use: connect multiple LANs across a city
  • Technology: Fiber optic cables
CAN

Campus Area Network

  • Use: connect multiple LANs in a campus
  • Technology: Ethernet, Wi-Fi 6
SAN

Storage Area Network

  • Use: connect servers to storage devices
  • Technology: Databases
POLAN

Passive Optical LAN

  • Use: LANs for tall, big buildings
  • Technology: IoT devices, sensors
EPN

Enterprise Private Network

  • Use: securely connect an organization
  • Technology: LANs, WANs, cloud services
VPN

Virtual Private Network

  • Use: secure internet connection
  • Technology: 1.1.1.1
SAN

System Area Network

  • Use: high-speed network for servers
  • Technology: fast backup & recovery
