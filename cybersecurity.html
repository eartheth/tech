Cybersecurity Tools (Antivirus & Anti-Spyware)

These tools help protect computers from malicious software, data theft, and online threats.

Overview

Antivirus software protects your computer by detecting, blocking, and removing viruses, worms, trojans, ransomware, and other harmful programs before they cause damage.

Features

  • Real-time scanning of files and programs
  • Automatic virus database updates
  • Threat quarantine and removal
  • Web protection and safe browsing
  • Email attachment scanning

Tips

  • Keep the antivirus updated daily.
  • Scan USB drives before opening files.
  • Avoid downloading software from unknown websites.
  • Run a full scan at least once a week.

Overview

Anti-spyware tools protect against software that secretly monitors your activity, steals passwords, records keystrokes, or collects personal information without your permission.

Features

  • Detection of tracking cookies and hidden spyware
  • Blocking keyloggers and screen recorders
  • Protection against data theft
  • Real-time monitoring of suspicious apps
  • Secure removal of spyware components

Tips

  • Do not click unknown pop-ups or ads.
  • Only install apps from trusted sources.
  • Avoid using cracked software—it often contains spyware.
  • Regularly check which apps have permission to access your data.
